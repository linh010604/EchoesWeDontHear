Our Mission
You didn't tell them.
They already knew.

Every scroll, pause, and tap leaves a trace. Algorithmic systems don't just collect your data — they infer who you are. They classify you as "high engagement," "low attention," or "high risk" without ever asking a single question.

The Echoes Foundation exists to make this invisible process visible. We believe you have the right to understand how digital systems see you — and the power to push back.

Our campaigns use the same tools that shape perception — social media, video, interactive content — to reveal the machinery behind the curtain.

How It Works
From passive consumer to active participant
01

Experience

Participants engage with what feels like a simple social media quiz. They answer casual questions about preferences and habits — nothing overtly personal.

02

Reveal

We show participants the surprisingly accurate inferences drawn from their answers — age range, interests, spending habits, even mood patterns — without ever asking directly.

03

Reflect

The "aha" moment opens a discussion about consent, transparency, and fairness. Participants leave understanding how algorithmic systems construct identity from behavioral signals.
